Experiences at
Bali Beach Hotel Sanur

Embark on a transformative journey of rediscovery at our sanctuary, where each moment reveals a treasure waiting to be uncovered. Immerse yourself in a realm of enchantment and embrace the extraordinary at Bali Beach Hotel Sanur.

Bali Beach Convention

Ideal for corporate travelers seeking sophisticated conference venues in Bali. Bali Beach Covention introduces the island's largest and most exceptional convention center, each of our event spaces provides a remarkable backdrop for your gatherings.

Agoong & Batoor Room

Experience elevated comfort in our Agoong and Batoor meeting rooms – a total of 22 spaces designed for your utmost convenience. Redefine your business meetings with luxury and seamless professionalism.

Roso Restaurant

Honoring the essence of Indonesia's best cuisine, culture, craftsmanship, and people, Roso, presents a fusion of traditional dishes. Explore Indonesian classics served alongside creative culinary creations that play with traditional spices and flavors sourced from across the archipelago.

Sutasoma

Discover serenity at Sutasoma Lounge, a haven where history meets relaxation. Indulge in coffee, tea, and a welcoming Jamu. This intimate space doubles as a library, featuring curated books on Bali, Indonesia's history, and more—an ideal retreat for every guest.

Svasana Spa

A harmonious sanctuary dedicated to the well-being of mind, body, and soul, we warmly welcome you to embark on a transformative wellness journey. Our array of services, rooted in local wisdom, is designed to engage each of your five senses—sound, sight, scent, taste, and touch—creating a holistic and rejuvenating experience.

Fitness Center

Our fitness center features advanced equipment, providing you with access to the latest tools for your workout needs. Operating 24 hours a day, our fitness facility is designed to accommodate your schedule, allowing you the flexibility to exercise at your convenience.  

Swimming Pool

Surrounded by lush tropical gardens and offering breathtaking views of the Sanur Beach, our impressive 60-meter swimming pool provides an ideal setting to swim laps or a refreshing dip at your convenience.

Kids Club

Our kids club enhances the family-friendly atmosphere at Bali Beach Hotel Sanur, making it an ideal destination for quality time with your loved ones.
